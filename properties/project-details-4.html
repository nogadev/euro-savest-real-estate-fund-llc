Amsterdam - De Kameleon

De Kameloen was built in 2012 and provides 1 to 4 bedroom apartments for rent in Weesperkarspel. The apartments come in a variety of sizes ranging from 54 sq. m to 137 sq. m offering you more than enough living space for your needs. Some apartments come with equipped with dishwashers, stunning laminate flooring, a Wi-Fi thermostat, rich white cabinets with modern silver handles and also come with balconies.

The building offers you a unique rooftop park for you to enjoy, as well as available indoor parking. Anchored by a retail mall at the bottom of the building you will conveniently have access to grocery stores, a gym, and restaurants to name a few.
