Chopinlaan is a gorgeous mid-rise building located in the majestic village of Doorwerth in the Province of Gelderland. The apartments for rent in Doorwerth offer you more than enough living space for you needs ranging in size from 53 to 98 sq. m. With 1 bedrooom up to 3 bedroom units available all are equipped with central heating, open concept layouts and a balcony. For your convenience some suites also come with extra storage space.

Chopinlaan is surrounded by lush trees with an abundance of trails nearby for you to enjoy. Located close to grocery stores, a sports complex, schools and several parks. There are several bus stops within walking distance and the N225 and A50 motorways are a short drive away. Arnhem and Nijmegen are respectively 30 and 45 minutes away by public transport and only 15 and 20 minutes by car.
